Welcome to grabb a pizza, where every slice tells a story of passion, tradition, and community. Our journey began with a simple dream: to bring the authentic flavors of Italy to our neighborhood, creating a place where families and friends could gather to enjoy great food and even better company.

OUR STORY

It all started in 2016, when our founder decided to turn a lifelong love for pizza into a reality. Inspired by the rich culinary heritage of Naples, the birthplace of pizza, Founder traveled to Italy to learn the art of pizza making from the masters themselves. After months of perfecting recipes and techniques, Founder returned home, ready to share these authentic flavors with our community.

OUR VISION

At grabb a pizza, our vision is to create a warm and welcoming space where everyone can experience the joy of authentic, handcrafted pizza. We aim to be more than just a restaurant; we strive to be a cornerstone of the community, a place where people come together to share delicious food, celebrate special moments, and build lasting memories.
